Tips to Spot Fake News

1. Check the Source

Investigate the website, its 'About Us' section, and contact information. Reputable news organizations provide detailed information about their editorial staff.

2. Examine the Evidence

Trustworthy news stories will include multiple sources, particularly for controversial claims. Be skeptical of stories lacking direct quotes or official reports.

3. Consider the Date

Old stories sometimes resurface and are shared as current events. Always check the publication date before sharing.

4. Watch for Emotional Language

Excessive use of capital letters, dramatic language, or urgent calls to action are often signs of misleading content.
